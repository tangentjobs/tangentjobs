Who We Are

Founded 27 years ago in the UAE, Tangent Group is a trusted recruitment agency specializing in overseas and local manpower supply. We connect human potential to the power of business advisory for clients and candidates.

We serve organizations across all industry sectors with 35,000 employees deployed in UAE and GCC countries. Our reputation is built on integrity, professionalism, and a global network of talent partners.
