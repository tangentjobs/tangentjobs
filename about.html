About Tangent Group

Founded 27 years ago in the UAE, Tangent Group is a trusted recruitment agency specializing in overseas and local manpower supply. Our team provides strategic business consulting, application services, technology solutions, and management services. We have extensive experience in successfully executing HR and Recruitment projects throughout the UAE and other GCC countries including Oman, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, and Kuwait. 35,000 employees across the UAE in various industries. Our reputation is built on integrity, professionalism, and a global network of talent partners.
